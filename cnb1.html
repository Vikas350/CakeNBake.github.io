Safety

As we immerse ourselves in guidance from federal, state, and local agencies, we have come together with industry peers, vendors, and experts to collect best practices to ensure the health and safety of all our guests and team members as we reopen our doors. Our safety plan is focused on six critical steps.

Wellness checks

We take steps to reduce exposure by implementing daily wellness checks through Covid-19 Health questionnaires and temperature checks for our team members. We require all guests to be symptom-free when joining us.

Use of PPE

We provide all team members with the recommended PPE, train them in the proper use and care of the PPE, and ensure compliance.
All guests are required to wear a protective face covering when not eating or drinking.

Enhanced Sanitisation

We have developed, trained, and implemented enhanced sanitation procedures across all areas of the restaurant in addition to constant monitoring via checklists.
We have implemented rigorous handwashing protocols requiring our teams to wash their hands for at least 20 seconds. Our employees must wash their hands after touching any surface.
We have intensified our commitment to providing clean and sanitized utensils, flatware, china, and menus to all guests and have made single-use disposable options available upon request.

Our story

I founded Cake n bake in 2009 after a failed attempt to find a vegan birthday cake for my oldest son who was turning 2. My grandmother who was my inspiration and an awesome baker gave me a couple recipes that I adapted and made vegan. I started baking out of my home kitchen and left my corporate job after I found a couple of restaurants where I could sell our cupcakes. Today Cake n Bake can be found in many wholesale locations around major cities like Mumbai , Delhi , kolkata and many more.
